Controls and Parametrization
 Animals are generated, the get hungry and die if they do not eat. They also prefer moving in flocks. The First slider controls the herd size and the second slider controls targetting. (Full -> Mouse Pointer. Zero -> Automatic (At this moment this moves target to center, in future it will basically imply free will.) )
